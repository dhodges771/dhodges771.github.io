Latest Updates

Brookhaven National Lab

We collaborate with BNL Scientists on cadmium zinc telluride (CZT) X-ray and gamma-ray radiation detection and Compton Imaging through the the Visiting Faculty and NSF LSAMP programs. Collaboration is with the Nonprofileration and National Security group, and with the University of Michigan's UMImaging group. We also work with Scientists at the Center for Functional Nanomaterials (CFN) in probing perovskites using PL, XPS and AFM techniques.

NREL

We participated in NREL's Hands-On Photovoltaics Experience (HOPE) program, working with NREL's Scientists, learning from about perovskites and photovoltaics. We also participated in NREL's Faculty Development workshop learning new approaches and best practices in teaching PV from leaders in the PV community.

El Paso Electric Company

The Renewable and Emergent Technologies Team at El Paso Electric Company gave a tour of their EPEC Stanton Tower Solar System to a large group of students from our Renewable Energy and Energy Sustainabilty courses. The EPEC team also gave a state-of-art presentation about EPEC's renewable programs and projects. This collaboration contiunues with plans to tour the new 3 MW solar facility.
